The Heritage of the Bean

Our Velvet Oat Latte begins in the high-altitude forests of Sidamo, Ethiopia. These heirloom beans are hand-harvested by local cooperatives and undergo a meticulous honey-processing method, preserving the fruit's natural sweetness.

Roasted to a precise "Medium-Light" profile, we extract notes of wild jasmine, stone fruit, and a lingering cocoa finish that pairs seamlessly with our creamy house-made oat milk.
